# Decision Tree

An algorithm that breaks down the problem into a series of simple questions until a decision is reached.

<img src="/ml-lesson-2-summary/assets/image-12.png" alt="image-12.png" width="666" height="417" />

At each node a question is asked about the data.
The answer determines which direction to continue.
At the end a prediction is obtained.

**Every path in the tree is a series of decisions leading to a result**
