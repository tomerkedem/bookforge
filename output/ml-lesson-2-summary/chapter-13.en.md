# Random Forest

An algorithm based on a large number of decision trees working together.

<img src="/ml-lesson-2-summary/assets/image-13.png" alt="image-13.png" width="546" height="344" />

Instead of one tree, the model builds many different trees,
where each tree learns a different part of the data.

Each tree gives its own prediction,
and the final result is obtained by combining all predictions.

In classification, a decision is made by majority,
and in numerical prediction, an average is calculated.

**The model doesn't rely on one tree, but on the wisdom of the crowd**
