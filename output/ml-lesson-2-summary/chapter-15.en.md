# Unsupervised Learning - Intuition

An algorithm that works with data without labels.

<img src="/ml-lesson-2-summary/assets/image-15.png" alt="image-15.png" width="507" height="185" />

The model doesn't receive correct answers, it receives data without labels,
and tries to understand the structure of the data by itself.

It looks for patterns and similarities between points,
and creates a division or new representation of the data.
