# Types of Problems in Supervised Learning

**In supervised learning there are two main types of problems:**

1. **Classification**
**The goal is to assign to a category**
For example, distinguishing between types of fruit or identifying whether an email is spam

2. **Regression**
**The goal is to predict a numerical value**
For example, predicting income or sales forecast

<img src="/ml-lesson-2-summary/assets/image-09.png" alt="image-09.png" width="710" height="263" />

**The difference between them depends on the type of result:**

When the result is a number, it's regression

When the result is a group, it's classification
