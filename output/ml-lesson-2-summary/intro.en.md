# Introduction

This lesson presents the fundamentals of Machine Learning within the broader context of Artificial Intelligence, aiming to build a gradual understanding of the field.

Our role as AI Engineers, and by analogy, is to take the engine and build a car from it. And to do that, **we must** understand the wrapper.

**We will learn:**

• What AI actually is, where Machine Learning fits within it, and why we use it

• What the big picture looks like before diving into the algorithms themselves

• How to use existing models and integrate them into production-ready products

The goal here is not to learn formulas or implementations, but to understand how to think correctly about Machine Learning.

The field seems complex at first glance, but once you understand the big picture, everything starts to connect.
